FAQs

What is TradingLeagues and how does it work?

TradingLeagues is a skills-based, fantasy-gaming platform for stocks and cryptocurrencies. Choose from four league formats with multiple games to play and enjoy an immersive experience of the financial markets. You can find out more about the games here.

How old do I need to be to play on TradingLeagues?

TradingLeagues is a game for all age groups, but only adults over 18 can withdraw any winnings on the platform.

What games can I play on TradingLeagues?

TradingLeagues has 4 exciting game formats: Battle Leagues, Target Leagues, Selection Leagues, and Classic Leagues. You can find out more about the games here.

What personal information does TradingLeague require?

TradingLeagues requires your phone number to create an account. If you wish to withdraw your winnings, you must verify your PAN and map your bank account details.

How do I download and install the app?

There are three ways to download the app:

  1. Directly on an Android mobile phone:

    • Visit www.tradingleagues.in
    • Tap the 'Download app' button.
    • Once the app is downloaded, tap to open it.
    • Tap 'Install' and sign up on TradingLeagues.

  2. Visit the Google Play Store.

    • Search for ‘TradingLeagues - Play stock games, and win cash'.
    • Tap on the 'Install' button and sign up on TradingLeagues.

  3. Directly on an iOS mobile phone:

    • Visit the App Store.
    • Search for ‘TradingLeagues - Play stock games, and win cash'.
    • Tap on the 'Get' button and sign up on TradingLeagues.

Do I need to pay to use TradingLeagues?

You can choose between free and paid games to play on TradingLeagues. Players are required to pay an entry fee to participate in the paid game. You can pay this from your winnings, cash balance, or Rain credits.

Do I need trading experience to use TradingLeagues?

No, you do not need any sort of trading experience to play on TradingLeagues. TradingLeagues is one of the best ways to be introduced to the financial markets. So, if you’re a newcomer to the markets, start with Battle Leagues, progress to Target Leagues, and climb up the ladder with Selection Leagues and Classic Leagues.

Is TradingLeagues secure?

Your privacy and data are our top priority. You can be rest assured that we do everything we can to protect your information and secure your experience. Tap here to read more about our privacy policy.

How do I open an account?

Just open the TradingLeagues app and enter your mobile number (keep in mind that your mobile number will be linked to your TradingLeagues account and cannot be changed later on). On your first login, you will receive an OTP on the mobile number you have entered on the app. Enter this OTP to verify your sign-in. You can choose an avatar name and image for your account.

Why am I unable to play games from my region/state?

As per the laws, real money gaming is not allowed in Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Odisha, Telangana, Nagaland, Meghalaya, and Sikkim. So, if you're from these states, you wouldn't be able to play on TradingLeagues.

What happens if I am traveling to a region/state that doesn’t have TradingLeagues?

To comply with the laws of certain states like Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Odisha, Telangana, Nagaland, Meghalaya, and Sikkim, you may not be able to use TradingLeagues when you are traveling to these states. You can resume playing on the app once you’re back in a state that allows real-money gaming.

I am unable to get an OTP/PIN.

You should receive your OTP (One Time Password) within one minute of requesting it.
Kindly ensure:

  • That the number you have entered is correct
  • You have a network strong enough to receive an SMS
If you still don’t receive the OTP, kindly contact your network service provider.
